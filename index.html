Our Services

Services

On-Forwarding & Customs Clearing

Seamless on-forwarding of shipments from ports or hubs to final destination.

Full customs clearing services for imports and exports, including documentation, tariff classification, SARS compliance, EDI submissions, permits, and regulatory support to minimise delays and ensure smooth clearance.

Air Freight

Fast, reliable domestic and international air freight solutions for time-sensitive and high value cargo.

Services include consolidation, next-flight-out options, dedicated charters, door-to-door delivery, and full shipment tracking. Suitable for general cargo, perishables, and specialised shipments.

Same-Day Deliveries

Prompt same-day collection and delivery service for urgent shipments of any size or weight.

Ideal for critical documents, medical supplies, spare parts, and time-sensitive commercial goods, with flexible routing and dedicated resources to guarantee speed and reliability

Local Transportation & Distribution

Efficient local & regional road transportation.

Including dedicated fleet options, last-mile delivery, point-to-point hauls, and scheduled distribution routes across Gauteng and South Africa. Focused on reliability, safety, and cost optimisation for retail, manufacturing, and wholesale clients.
