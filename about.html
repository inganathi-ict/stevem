About Us

Founded in 2025, SteveM Group is a specialised logistics and freight solutions company built on nearly two decades of industry expertise. The company was established following extensive experience within leading logistics environments, including Courierit SA (a division of RTT Group), bringing a strong foundation of operational knowledge and market insight.

SteveM Group is committed to delivering efficient, reliable, and client-focused logistics solutions across South Africa. With a deep understanding of supply chain dynamics, the company focuses on optimising operations, improving efficiency, and providing tailored services that meet the evolving needs of its clients.

Driven by professionalism, industry experience, and a results-oriented approach, SteveM Group aims to be a trusted partner in logistics and freight management.
