About Us

Steve Mabulana Founder & CEO – SteveM Group Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa

With over 19 years of extensive experience in logistics operations and management, I am the Founder and CEO of SteveM Group, a specialised logistics and freight solutions company established in 2025.

I launched SteveM Group after taking an early retirement package from Courierit SA (a division of RTT Group) in December 2024, bringing deep industry expertise and a fresh, client-focused approach to the market.

My career has equipped me with strong skills in supply chain optimisation, operational efficiency, team leadership, and delivering reliable logistics solutions across South Africa.
